About Me

Hi, I'm Ikram, a Data Scientist with a primary focus on Computer Vision. Currently, I'm involved in various projects at CEFAS, ranging from advanced segmentation models using vision transformers to good old automated data cleaning.

I attribute much of my progress as a data scientist to my academic background in Aerospace Engineering (BEng) and Space Engineering (MSc). After my Masters, I briefly worked as a data science consultant at Rockborne before tranisitioning into a fully fledged data scientist at Cefas at the end of 2023.

I'm always on the lookout for a good idea, and sometimes the best ideas come from the most random conversations. Feel free to contact me through LinkedIn or email.
